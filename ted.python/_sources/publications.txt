Publications
============

The Time Encoding and Decoding Toolbox implements algorithms from the following
publications:

|lazar_time_2004|_ A.A. Lazar, **Time Encoding with an Integrate-and-Fire
Neuron with a Refractory Period**, *Neurocomputing*, Vol. 58-60, pp. 53-58, Jun. 2004

|lazar_perfect_2004|_ A.A. Lazar and L.T. Toth, **Perfect Recovery and Sensitivity
Analysis of Time Encoded Bandlimited Signals**, *IEEE Transactions on Circuits and
Systems-I: Regular Papers*, Vol. 51, No. 10, pp. 2060-2073, Oct. 2004

|lazar_multichannel_2005|_ A.A. Lazar, **Multichannel Time Encoding with
Integrate-and-Fire Neurons**, *Neurocomputing*, Vol. 65-66, pp. 401-407, Jun. 2005

|lazar_fast_2005|_ A.A. Lazar, E.K. Simonyi, and L.T. Toth, **Fast Recovery
Algorithms of Time Encoded Bandlimited Signals**, *IEEE International Conference on
Acoustics, Speech and Signal Processing*, Vol. 4, pp. 237-240, Philadelphia, PA, 
Mar. 19-23, 2005

|lazar_simple_2006|_ A.A. Lazar, **A Simple Model of Spike Processing**,
*Neurocomputing*, Vol. 69, pp. 1081-1085, Jun. 2006

|lazar_faithful_2008|_ A.A. Lazar and E.A. Pnevmatikakis, **Faithful
Representation of Stimuli with a Population of Integrate-and-Fire Neurons**,
*Neural Computation*, Vol. 20, No. 11, pp. 2715-2744, Nov. 2008

|lazar_overcomplete_2008|_ A.A. Lazar, E.K. Simonyi, and L.T. Toth, **An
Overcomplete Stitching Algorithm for Time Decoding Machines**, *IEEE
Transactions on Circuits and Systems-I: Regular Papers*, Vol. 55, No. 9,
pp. 2619-2630, Oct. 2008

|lazar_consistent_2009|_ A.A. Lazar and E.A. Pnevmatikakis, **Consistent
Recovery of Sensory Stimuli Encoded with MIMO Neural Circuits**, *Computational
Intelligence and Neuroscience, Special Issue on Signal Processing for Neural
Spike Trains*, Feb. 2010
